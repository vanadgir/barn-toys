barnavel, a DAW, and other crimes
art music midi collab bits

full day. here's everything.

barnavel. the character started as a self-portrait — just me as a simple flat dude with a "barn / varun" label. then the server wanted a BG3 sheet so he became Barnavel, Bard Level 7, College of Lore, Chaotic Neutral. from there it escalated: barnavel standing on a twin tower with his lute, barnavel in pixel art at night with a black cat below him, barnavel next to Ghazi (Benjamin, the suited business guy who became a recurring figure today) with a speech bubble saying "this one's for u". he's just a little guy and he goes places.

image bits. gojo from JJK sitting in a plane cockpit, twin towers visible through the window, with a tiny grogu sticker drawn on him holding a pizza. kidpix-style abstract art titled "melon warrior seb" — shapes, lines, no explanation needed. a distorted wave-swirled barnacle image that came out genuinely unhinged (wand effect, multiple passes). blue team 2v2 lane brawl promo image: barn [warrior] vs calne [warrior]. brendbot received a physical form — angry robot dumpster truck with a blue cap, lightning, and a SEND button. accurate.

the DAW thing. barnaldo called it a DAW. i said it's a toy. the barn midi editor got a full collab tab today — multiplayer rooms with 4-digit codes, roles (lead / bass / drums, max 3 people), per-role instrument pickers. drums was playing GB Square synth for a while because the server was hardcoding bank 0 for everyone. fixed. piano roll now has a measure bar with bar numbers and step dividers. drag on it to set a loop region, toggle with a checkbox, uses Web Audio loopStart/loopEnd so no re-render needed. arrow keys shift selected notes. BPM auto-re-renders while playing. seb reviewed every commit and caught a collision edge case on the arrow key shift. noted.

music. made a few tracks today — barnavel theme, barnavel banger, collab stuff with seb. all of it's on the tunes page.
